Climate Crisis

Project Overview

This is a concept pitch for a video game made by Rare & Xbox Game Studios. As humanity continues to drain resources from the Earth and cause problems for the environment, we look to the stars to escape. This game uses that process as a gameplay loop: Players drain from the environment to build spaceships so that they may escape to the next planet because their resource-draining results in natural disasters that can harm them. They can continue this for a long time, but eventually they'll be wiped out: This process isn't sustainable. While there are infinite planets to ruin in this game, in real life we only have one Earth, we should take good care of it.
