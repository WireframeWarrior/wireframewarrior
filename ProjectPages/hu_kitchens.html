Hu Kitchens: Get Back To Human

Hu Kitchens aims to provide a healthier and more natural alternative to our favorite processed sweets. We noticed that their social media content is static and not too engaging. We wanted to correct this by creating something with some more sharing potential to create more traffic. We created this spot with the insight: It's hard to ditch our favorite snacks, but we acknowledge that they're unhealthy.
