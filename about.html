About me

I’m Luis Montalvo, a multimedia designer from Brooklyn. I’m a recent graduate from the Fashion Institute of Technology's Animation & Game Design program. I also have a BFA In Advertising & Digital Design and a minor in Film & Media. I love combining skills from different disciplines to create wonderful work. From copywriting, animation, to UX/UI Design, I’m always on the hunt for new things to learn to bring my visions to life. Lately, I’ve been learning programming languages so that I can turn my designs into playable video games using game engines like Unity and Unreal. I look forward to someday being an accomplished designer, and expanding upon my skills so that I can create truly awesome experiences.
